## Huckel Rule Connection

Aromatic stability requires 4n+2 pi electrons.
- n=1: **6** pi electrons = P₁ (benzene, most stable aromatic)
- The rule itself: 4×1+2 = 4+2 = tau(6)+phi(6) = 6 = P₁
