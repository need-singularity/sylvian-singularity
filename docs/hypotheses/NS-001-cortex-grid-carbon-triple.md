## Level 1: Carbon Z=6 = P₁

- Atomic number 6 = first perfect number
- 4 valence electrons = tau(6): enables tetravalent bonding
- Basis of ALL organic chemistry and ALL known life

## Level 2: Benzene C₆H₆

- 6 carbons + 6 hydrogens = 12 atoms = sigma(6)
- 6 pi electrons (Huckel aromatic stability)
- Foundation of aromatic chemistry

## Level 3: DNA Codon = 6 bits

- 64 codons = 2⁶ = 2^P₁
- log₂(64) = 6 = P₁ bits of information per codon
- 4 bases = tau(6), 3 bases per codon = P₁/phi
- 20 amino acids = sopfr(6) × tau(6) = 20

## Level 4: Cerebral Cortex = 6 layers

- Neocortex: exactly 6 layers (I through VI) in ALL mammals
- Layer IV (input) + Layer V (output) = 2 IO layers = phi(6)
- ~12 distinct cell types per cortical column = sigma(6)
- This is the physical substrate of consciousness

## Level 5: Grid Cells = 6-fold hexagonal (Nobel 2014)

- Grid cells fire in regular hexagonal patterns
- 6 nearest neighbors per firing vertex = P₁
- 60-degree angular spacing = 360/P₁
- Nobel Prize 2014: O'Keefe, May-Britt Moser, Edvard Moser
- Reason: hexagonal = optimal 2D packing (kissing number in 2D = 6)
