# Hypothesis MUSIC-067: Standard Dynamic Markings = P1 = 6

**Grade: 🟩 EXACT**

## Hypothesis

> Standard musical dynamics uses 6 = P1 primary levels:
> pp, p, mp, mf, f, ff.

## Background

Dynamic markings indicate loudness. The six standard levels from
pianissimo to fortissimo form the core dynamic vocabulary.

## Numerical Verification

| Marking | Name          | Level |
|---------|--------------|-------|
| pp      | Pianissimo   |   1   |
| p       | Piano        |   2   |
| mp      | Mezzo-piano  |   3   |
| mf      | Mezzo-forte  |   4   |
| f       | Forte        |   5   |
| ff      | Fortissimo   |   6   |

Standard levels = 6 = P1

## ASCII Diagram

```
  Volume:  soft ──────────────────── loud
           pp    p    mp    mf    f    ff
           1     2    3     4     5    6 = P1

  Extended: ppp  pp  p  mp  mf  f  ff  fff
            (adding extremes = 8 = 2*tau(6))
```

## Interpretation

6 standard dynamics = P1. Extended markings (ppp, fff) add 2 = phi(6).

## Limitations

- ppp and fff are common enough that "8 dynamics" is also defensible.
