## Limitations

1. **Directionality is interpretive**: The Obang-to-Platonic mapping proposed here (East→Tetrahedron
   etc.) is one of 5!=120 possible bijections. The choice is motivated by element associations
   but is not uniquely forced by mathematics.

2. **Small numbers law**: With only 5 solids and 5 directions, coincidences are expected.
   The appearance of 6 and 12 in the face/vertex/edge counts is constrained by the geometry
   of regular polyhedra, not freely variable.

3. **χ=2 is universal**: Every convex polyhedron satisfies χ=2, not just the five Platonic
   solids. The universality weakens the specificity of the parallel to σ₋₁(6)=2.

4. **Element mappings conflict**: Greek (Fire/Air/Water/Earth/Aether) and Chinese (Wood/Fire/
   Metal/Water/Earth) elemental systems do not share the same ontology. The cross-mapping
   introduces interpretive ambiguity.

5. **No empirical prediction**: This hypothesis does not currently generate testable predictions
   distinguishable from the null hypothesis. It is a structural observation.
