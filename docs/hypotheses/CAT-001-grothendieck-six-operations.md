> Grothendieck's six-functor formalism — the foundation of modern algebraic
> geometry and homological algebra — requires exactly P₁ = 6 operations,
> forming P₁/phi = 3 adjoint pairs.

## The Six Operations

| # | Operation | Notation | Role |
|---|-----------|----------|------|
| 1 | Inverse image | f* | Pull back |
| 2 | Direct image | f_* | Push forward |
| 3 | Exceptional inverse | f! | Shriek pullback |
| 4 | Proper direct | f_! | Compactly supported push |
| 5 | Tensor product | tensor | Monoidal structure |
| 6 | Internal Hom | Hom | Mapping space |

**Count = 6 = P₁**

## Three Adjoint Pairs = P₁/phi = 3

| Pair | Left adjoint | Right adjoint |
|------|-------------|---------------|

| 2 | f_! | f! |

**Pairs = 3 = P₁/phi(6) = 6/2**
