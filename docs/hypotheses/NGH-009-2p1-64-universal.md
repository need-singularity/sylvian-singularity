The I Ching and DNA use IDENTICAL mathematical structure:
- 2^P1 = 64 codewords
- P1 = 6 binary positions per codeword
- 2 symbols per position (yin/yang vs A-T/G-C pairs)

## Significance

Three of these (codons, I Ching, chess) were designed/evolved independently
across 3000+ years of human history and 3.5 billion years of evolution.
