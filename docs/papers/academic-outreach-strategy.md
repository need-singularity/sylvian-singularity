## 3. Presentation Strategy

**Lead with the MATH, not the model.**

The GZ model (G=D*P/I) is speculative. The mathematical results are provable and should be presented first:

1. phi*sigma=n*tau at n=6 — pure number theory, anyone can verify
2. I^I minimum at 1/e — elementary calculus
3. Singleton rates at n=6 = divisor reciprocals — coding theory fact

Present these as standalone mathematical observations. The GZ interpretation comes second, clearly labeled as a model/hypothesis rather than a proven theorem.

## 7. Verification Status Note

Per project policy: the Golden Zone (G=D*P/I) itself is simulation-based and lacks analytical proof.
All outreach materials must clearly distinguish:

- **Proven**: Pure mathematical results (phi*sigma=n*tau uniqueness, I^I minimum, Singleton rate correspondence)
- **Unverified model**: Golden Zone interpretation, G=D*P/I formula, consciousness continuity claims

Never present Golden Zone-dependent claims as "proven" in academic submissions.
