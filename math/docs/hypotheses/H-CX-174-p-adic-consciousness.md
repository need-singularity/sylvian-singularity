## Hypothesis Statement

> The p-adic valuations of n!=6!=720 at the primes of 6 are:
> v₂(6!) = 4 = τ(6) and v₃(6!) = 2 = φ(6).
> The p-adic absolute values |6!|_p = p^{-v_p} give:
