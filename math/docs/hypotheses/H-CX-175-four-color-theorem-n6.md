## Hypothesis Statement

> The Four Color Theorem: every planar graph is τ(6)=4 colorable.
> The chromatic number of the plane χ(plane) = 4 = τ. The chromatic
> polynomial P(K₆, k) at k=τ=4 gives the number of proper 4-colorings
> of the complete graph K₆ (which requires n=6 colors, but τ=4 suffices
> for planar graphs). The Petersen graph also has χ=τ=4.
